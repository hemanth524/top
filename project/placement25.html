PLACEMENTS

The institute has released the final placement report for the MBA batch 2021-23. The IIT Kanpur average package and highest package offered during MBA placements 2023 was INR 18.14 LPA and INR 24 LPA, respectively. Further, the lowest package and median package stood at INR 12.41 LPA and INR 17.25 LPA, respectively. Analytics emerged as the top recruiting sector. Barclays, HDFC Bank, and EXL, among others, were the leading companies that participated in IIT Kanpur MBA placements 2023
