PLACEMENTS

The highest international and domestic package offered during IIT Bombay placements 2023 stood at INR 3.67 crore per annum and INR 1.68 crore per annum, respectively. Further, the average package offered during IIT Bombay placements 2023 stood at INR 21.82 LPA. In addition, the overall placement rate recorded during IIT Bombay placements 2023 was 82%. Moreover, a total of 384 companies participated and 1,898 offers were made during IIT Bombay placements 2023. A total of 1,104 internship offers were made during IIT Bombay internships 2023. The top recruiters that visited the campus during IIT Bombay placements 2023 included Uber, Rubrik and Millennium.
